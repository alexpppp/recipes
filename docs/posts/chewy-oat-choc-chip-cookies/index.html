Chewy Oat Choc Chip Cookies

Baking Dessert Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 cup (or more) chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 165Â°C.
  2. Cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add the vanilla.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt until just combined.
  5. Mix in oats and chocolate chips.
  6. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto a lined baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 12 minutes, or until the edges are golden and centres are just set.
