Corn & Cos Lettuce Salad

Dinner Salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cobs corn
  • 1 cos lettuce
  • Shaved parmesan
  • Paprika and/or chilli powder

Method

  1. Pre-cook the corn, then char it on the BBQ for extra flavour.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together all dressing ingredients and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toss cos lettuce leaves with the dressing.
  4. Top with charred corn, shaved parmesan, and a sprinkle of paprika and/or chilli powder.
