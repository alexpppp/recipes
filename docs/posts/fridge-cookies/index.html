Fridge Cookies

Baking Snack

Ingredients

  • WET MIX
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 225g (1/2 pound) butter
  • DRY MIX
  • 3 cups oatmeal
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence

Method

  1. In a saucepan, combine sugar, cocoa, milk, and butter.
  2. Bring to a boil and cook for 3â€“5 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in oatmeal and vanilla essence.
  4. Drop spoonfuls onto a tray or baking sheet in small circular shapes.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour until set.
