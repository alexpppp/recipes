Cauliflower Base Pizza

Dinner

Ingredients

  • BASE
  • 1 medium-sized cauliflower, blended into rice-sized pieces
  • 1 cup cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp oregano or basil
  • Salt and pepper

To Top

  • (Choose your favourites)
  • Tomato paste
  • Caramelised onion
  • Pumpkin
  • Sundried tomato
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach
  • Olives
  • Jalapeños
  • Cream cheese
  • Pesto
  • Cheese to top

Method

  1. Blitz cauliflower into rice-sized pieces (in batches if needed).
  2. Steam for 15 minutes, then drain excess water.
  3. In a bowl, combine steamed cauliflower with cheese, eggs, herbs, salt and pepper.
  4. Press mixture onto a greased/lined tray to form a base about 1cm thick.
  5. Bake at 200°C for 25 minutes or until browning.
  6. Add desired toppings and return to oven for 10–15 minutes (grill for 2–3 minutes at the end if cheese isn’t browning).
  7. Serve with knife and fork — it's a soft base!
