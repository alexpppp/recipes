Baking

Ingredients

  • 200g butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa
  • 2 cups cornflakes

Icing

  • 50g butter
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup icing sugar
  • 3 tbsp cocoa

Method

  6. Bake for about 9 minutes.
