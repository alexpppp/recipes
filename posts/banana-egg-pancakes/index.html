Banana Egg ‘Pancakes’

Snack

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 Tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Method

  1. Heat a non-stick pan.
  2. Mash bananas until smooth.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs.
  4. Combine mashed bananas with eggs, then mix in flour and cinnamon.
  5. Lightly grease the pan and cook small dollops of the mixture.
  6. Serve with any toppings you like.
