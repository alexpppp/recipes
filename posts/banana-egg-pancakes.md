---
title: Banana Egg ‘Pancakes’
tags: ["snack"]
imgFile: "banana-egg-pancakes.jpg"
date: 2018-06-03
ingredients:
  - 2 bananas
  - 4 eggs
  - 3 Tbsp flour
  - 1 tsp cinnamon
method:
  - Heat a non-stick pan.
  - Mash bananas until smooth.
  - In a separate bowl, whisk eggs.
  - Combine mashed bananas with eggs, then mix in flour and cinnamon.
  - Lightly grease the pan and cook small dollops of the mixture.
  - Serve with any toppings you like.
